CHeBA NiL Software (CNS)

CHeBA (Centre for Healthy Brain Ageing) NiL (Neuroimaging Lab) Software (CNS) was developed by Dr. Jiyang Jiang and Dr. Wei Wen. The aim was to pipeline neuroimaging data processing. There are multiple modules available in CNS:

White Matter Lesions

White matter Lesions (WML) are an ischaemic biomarker of cerebral small vessel disease. We developed UBO Detector - a fully automated, cluster-based pipeline for segmenting WML. In order to map WML masks to strategic white matter (WM) tracts, we then developed the TOolbox for Probablistic MApping of Lesions (TOPMAL), which can now be used for mapping any lesions in DARTEL space to John Hopkins University WM Tractography Atlas (JHU-WM Atlas) or Harvard-Oxford Subcortical Atlas.

We also pipelined BIANCA (Griffanti et al. 2016) to automate the whole processing procedures.

Cerebral Blood Flow

We pipelined both oxford_asl (version 3.9.13) and ASLtbx2 for an automated processing of ASL data. At the moment, these pipelines can only process single TI ASL data (both pCASL and PASL).
